Problem

Due to the Lack of Equipments which most hospitals need to carry out medical operation,poor electricity in the country, specialized expertriates,Avoidable mistakes which led to deaths of patients and so on has made the citizens of Nigeria lack trust in the medical system of the Nation which had led to a lot of people travelling outside the country to get the best medical care, improving other nations economy

Solution

To achieve a better medical system in Nigeria, we need to gain the trust of the citizens back which is creating a feedback platform for the patients,an unbiase review system which will maintain standards of hospitals in Nigera.Medical Complain System is one of the offical website of the Nigeria Medical Association which aims at improving the medical system in Nigeria and ensuring a better patient experience in Hospitals. This is achieved by enabling individuals sign up on our website and give feedbacks on their experience in Hospitals. Also in other to ensure a better experience in hospitals, individuals can peruse through our website and discover other features such as: Search for a Doctor or Hospital, Blog Post, Health tips e.t.c

Value Propositon

Our Online Complain System will provide a platform for Doctors,Any medical practitionals,Hospitals to be inlighn with mondern medical practices and modern day technology where they can get feedbacks from their patients to better enhance their services, as well as provide patients with an open and unbiased reviews
